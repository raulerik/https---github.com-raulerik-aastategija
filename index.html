29 Years of Unity and Athletic Achievements

The Baltic States Sport Games of Vocational Schools have been a significant and unifying event for students and employees over the past 29 years.

With participants from Latvia, Lithuania, and Estonia, the games have become a platform to foster friendship, athletic achievement, and cooperation among young people, educators, and educational institutions.
